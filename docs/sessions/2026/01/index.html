Journal Club – January 2026

Critical appraisal and application of perioperative evidence

Session Overview

This Journal Club session focuses on evaluating contemporary evidence relevant to anesthesia and perioperative care. Emphasis will be placed on study design, effect sizes, applicability, and clinical translation.

Learning Objectives

  1. Critically assess internal validity and sources of bias.
  2. Interpret effect sizes and confidence intervals.
  3. Distinguish statistical significance from clinical relevance.
  4. Apply evidence to real-world perioperative decision-making.
