Anesthesia During Pregnancy & Child Neurodevelopment

A curated, resident-focused review of recent evidence (last 10 years)

Session Details

Topic

Anesthesia in Pregnancy & Child Development

Audience

Anesthesiology Residents

Format

Comparative appraisal of cohorts, meta-analyses & reviews

Faculty Leads

TBA

Learning Objectives

  • Critically appraise evidence linking prenatal anesthesia/analgesia exposure to child neurodevelopment.
  • Distinguish association vs causation in observational designs.
  • Contrast registry outcomes with prospective neuropsychological testing.
  • Apply findings to counseling and perioperative decision-making.
  • Identify and discuss confounding by indication and familial factors.
